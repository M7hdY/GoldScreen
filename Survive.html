SURVIVE is about two ordinary people, Jane, and Paul, who are the only survivors of a plane crash that isolates them on a remote snow-covered mountain. Soon this unlikely duo will have to find a way to get back to society, all while Jane struggles with her own personal demons and Paul does everything he can to keep her safe.
